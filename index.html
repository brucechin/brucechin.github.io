About Me

I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University with a B.Eng. in Computer Software Engineering in 2019 summer. I spent one year at Northwestern CS and then transferred to UC Santa Barbara to continue my Ph.D. in data system. I currently work closely with Prof. Shumo Chu. My main research interest lies in applying modern cryptography techniques as well as trusted hardware like Intel SGX into data analytics system on untrusted cloud to obtain more security and privacy guarantee. Life is about doing tradeoff, you can't expect to grasp security & performance and win them all:)
